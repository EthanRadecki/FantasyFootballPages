Draft Slot Analysis

Analyzing draft position success and controlling for manager quality (2020-2025)

How It Works

Dominance Score measures how much better or worse a team scored compared to the league average each week. Positive scores mean consistent outperformance. Manager-Adjusted Performance compares how a manager actually performed from each draft slot versus what we'd expect based on their career average. A positive value means that slot tends to produce better results than the manager's baseline.
