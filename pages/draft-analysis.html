Draft slot success, player hit rates, and the greatest steals in league history (2020-2025)

How It Works

Dominance Score measures how much better or worse a team scored compared to the league average each week. Positive scores mean consistent outperformance. Manager-Adjusted Performance compares how a manager actually performed from each draft slot versus what we would expect based on their career average. A positive value means that slot tends to produce better results than the manager's baseline.

Methodology

A pick is a hit if the player finished in the top 24 (WR/RB) or top 7 (QB/TE) at their position in PPR points per game for that season, calibrated to the league size and measured relative to that year's scoring environment. Late-round steals are ranked by points above position average so skill positions are not compared to QBs on raw scoring. Data covers 2020-2024. Minimum 4 games played to qualify.
