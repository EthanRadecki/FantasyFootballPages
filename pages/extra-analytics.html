Schedule Luck
Who has the schedule been kind to?
How It Works

Each week, every manager's score is compared to the league median. If you scored above the median, you "should have" won that week regardless of your actual opponent. The difference between your actual wins and these expected wins is your schedule luck. Positive means the schedule gifted you wins; negative means it took them away.

Seasonal Analysis
Which quarter of the season matters most?
Methodology

A logistic regression tested which part of the season best predicts playoff qualification, using average points scored each quarter as independent variables. The model achieved an AUC of 0.83, indicating strong predictive accuracy. Q2 (Weeks 4-6) and Q4 (Weeks 10-13) carry the largest coefficients, while Q3 (Weeks 7-9) is the weakest predictor.

Historical Similarity
2025 playoff teams matched to league history.
About This Analysis

For each 2025 playoff team, a weighted Euclidean distance algorithm identified the three most statistically similar teams from 2020-2024 across seven performance features. Features are normalized via StandardScaler, with custom weights reflecting each metric's relative importance to playoff performance.
