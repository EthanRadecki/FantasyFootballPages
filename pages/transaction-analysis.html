Transaction Analysis

Every draft pick, waiver claim, drop, and trade across six seasons (2020-2025), sourced directly from ESPN's transaction log.

About This Data

This covers everything after the draft: adds, drops, trades, and weekly lineup decisions. Six seasons (2020-2025) add up to 1,360 draft picks, 2,162 waiver/free agent adds, 2,165 drops, and 871 trade line-items across 150 real trades. Thomas Sullivan and William Serafin (2020 only) are excluded, same as the rest of the site. Playoff weeks only count real bracket games. Consolation weeks are excluded.
