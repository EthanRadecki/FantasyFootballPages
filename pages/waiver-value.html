Waiver Value

Points produced per week rostered, for every waiver and free agent pickup (2020-2025).

Methodology

The league used FAAB bidding only in 2025. From 2020-2024 it ran on reverse-order waiver priority, where a successful claim sends you to the back of the line. Since "cost" isn't comparable across those two systems, this page measures pure output instead: total points scored by every pickup, divided by total weeks rostered. A stint ends at the next drop or trade-away, whichever comes first. Thomas Sullivan and William Serafin (2020 only) are excluded, same as the rest of the site.
