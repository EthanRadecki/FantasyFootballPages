Surplus Value Index

How much value did each manager extract from every pick, relative to what that slot should produce? (2020-2025)

How It Works

For each drafted player, we find all players taken within 3 picks of that spot at the same position across all seasons and compute their average PPR/game. That's the expected value. Subtract expected from actual and you get the surplus. Stack all surpluses for a manager and you get their draft grade. Picks where the player appeared in fewer than 8 games count as 0 surplus, not negative, since injuries are random and shouldn't tank a manager's career grade. Stats are sourced directly from ESPN fantasy scoring to match your exact league settings. Data covers all six seasons, 2020-2025.
