Surplus Value Index

How much value did each manager extract from every pick, relative to what that slot should produce? (2020-2025)

How It Works

Each pick is scored using position-relative value (PRV) -- how a player performed versus the average starter at their position that season (top 14 for QB/TE, top 28 for RB/WR in a 14-team league). That PRV is then compared against the cross-position average at that draft slot, capturing opportunity cost. A TE who outperforms the TE baseline by 5 points gets the same credit as a WR who outperforms the WR baseline by 5 points. Round weights apply: 1.0 in rounds 1-3, 0.85 in rounds 4-7, 0.70 in rounds 8-12, 0.55 in rounds 13-16. Picks with fewer than 8 games played are injury-neutralized to zero surplus.
