Lineup Efficiency

Actual points started vs. the best possible lineup each roster could have set, week by week (2020-2025).

Methodology

The optimal lineup is the best possible score from a manager's full roster that week. It follows real slot rules: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 FLEX, 1 D/ST, 1 K. IR players are excluded. Bye weeks and consolation weeks are excluded too. One exception: a manager who skipped setting a lineup after elimination (since banned) is excluded from the efficiency average but still counts as a missed win, since he'd have won with any real lineup.
