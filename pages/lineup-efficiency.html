Lineup Efficiency

Actual points started vs. the best possible lineup each roster could have set, week by week (2020-2025).

Methodology

The optimal lineup is the highest-scoring combination available from a manager's full roster that week, honoring real slot rules: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 FLEX (RB/WR/TE), 1 D/ST, 1 K. Players in the IR slot are excluded from the optimal calculation, since they were flagged unplayable that week. Bye weeks (relevant in 2020's 15-team format) and consolation/loser-bracket playoff weeks are excluded entirely by joining against real matchup results — only weeks with a genuine regular-season or bracket-playoff game count. One historical exception is flagged separately: a manager who did not set a lineup after already being eliminated (a since-banned scenario) is excluded from the efficiency-gap average but still counted toward missed wins, since he genuinely would have won with any real lineup.
