Dimension Guide

Early RB% / WR% -- share of rounds 1-3 picks going to that position. QB/TE Timing -- average round of first QB or TE pick (lower = earlier). Skill Diversity -- how evenly spread picks are across RB/WR/QB/TE (higher = more balanced). K/DST Patience -- how many rounds later than league average a manager waits to draft their first kicker and defense (positive = more patient, negative = reaches early). Surplus -- average surplus value per pick, measuring how much better or worse drafted players performed relative to their slot expectations.

Draft Archetypes
K-means clustering groups manager-seasons into four distinct drafting styles.
Important Note

This analysis reflects draft-day decisions only. It does not capture in-season waiver additions, trades, injuries, or lineup management. Two managers in the same archetype may have very different season outcomes due to factors that happen after the draft. Use this as a lens on drafting philosophy, not a complete picture of manager quality.
